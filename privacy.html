Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

Little Stars is a babysitting service operated by Naomi, based in Peterborough, United Kingdom.

What Data We Collect

When you contact us via the booking form, we collect your name, contact details, and booking preferences. This information is used solely to arrange and provide our babysitting services.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. Analytics cookies are only loaded after you provide consent via the cookie banner. You can reject cookies and the site will function normally without them.

Cookies used when accepted:

  • _ga — Google Analytics identifier (expires: 2 years)
  • _ga_* — Google Analytics session data (expires: 2 years)

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: Analytics data is processed by Google with IP anonymisation enabled. See Google's privacy policy.

GitHub Pages: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. All data is collected from parents or guardians making bookings.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to processing and to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

Data Retention

Booking and contact data is retained only as long as needed to provide our services and is deleted within 12 months of your last booking. Analytics data is retained for 14 months in Google Analytics.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top indicates the last revision.
